Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 14, 2025

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or user information. This extension operates entirely locally on your device.

Permissions Explained

Our extension requires the following permissions:

  • "downloads" - Required to save images to your computer's Downloads folder
  • "storage" - Used only locally to remember your selection preferences within the current browsing session
  • "pinterest.com" - Required to detect and interact with pins on Pinterest pages
  • "pinimg.com" - Required to access Pinterest's image servers for downloading

No Tracking

This extension does not use cookies, analytics, tracking pixels, or any tracking technologies. We do not monitor your browsing activity.

No External Servers

All processing happens locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers or third parties.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs.
